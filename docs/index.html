Technical and vocational secondary education

ISIC Rev.5 8522

An open-source, forkable business for independent vocational schools and trade-training organizations. This actor governs the complete student lifecycle: enrollment intake, jurisdiction assessment, academic-integrity screening, workplace-safety-training screening, certification finalization, and graduation finalization.

Built on a Curriculum Safeguarding Governor that ensures no certification or graduation is finalized without human sign-off, with an immutable audit ledger tracking every decision. The governor enforces that workplace-safety-training certification is confirmed before any trade certification is granted — a non-negotiable safeguard for hands-on practicum work.

Deploy as a self-hosted instance or managed service, keeping student data under your control and audit trails fully transparent. No SaaS vendor lock-in. No fabricated credentials.
